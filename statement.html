Statement

I paint a series of different subjects, I am obsessed with beautiful art. And more interested in conveying an objective art, rather than illustrating paintings, influenced by personal feelings or opinions. Using oil paint I contrast the subtlety of the subjects in array way with bold colors and a morass of thick textures and other different elements unified organically.

I look at ordinary things that had something extra, reliable, and designed to convince my liberal vision. Trusting my intuition, embracing the right value, capturing each stroke with choppy and deftly brushstrokes that combined several shades of each color to make a vibrant, natural whole. These inseparable aspects are the keys to this new body of work.
